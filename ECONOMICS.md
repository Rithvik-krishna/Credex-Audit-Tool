# Unit Economics & Financial Model — AI Spend Audit Tool

This document outlines the commercial value of captured leads for **Credex**, Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) structures, conversion funnel dynamics, and the business math required to scale this tool into a $1,000,000 ARR channel.

---

## 1. What is a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

**Credex's Business Model:** Credex aggregates startup API consumption and purchases bulk institutional credits from AI API vendors (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini) at deep discounts (e.g. 30% volume discounts). Credex then passes a portion of these discounts to startups (e.g. 15% discount) and retains the remaining **15% spread as net margin**.

### Lead Value Mathematics:
Let us calculate the Lifetime Value (LTV) of a converted high-savings lead:
- **Average Startup API Spend:** $5,000 / month ($60,000 / year).
- **Credex Net Margin Spread:** 15%.
- **Monthly Net Revenue per Customer:** $5,000 * 0.15 = **$750 / month**.
- **Annual Net Revenue per Customer:** $750 * 12 = **$9,000 / year**.
- **Customer Lifetime (Retention):** 24 months (2 years).
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** $9,000 * 2 = **$18,000 (Gross Margin)**.

Therefore, every single startup that switches its API spend to Credex is worth **$18,000 in Net Margin LTV**.

## 3. The Lead Conversion Funnel

To convert a cold visitor into a paying Credex client, we optimize a multi-stage funnel:

```
100% - Cold Visitors (1,000 people)
  │
  ▼ (40% conversion - clear messaging, interactive sliders)
 40% - Audits Completed (400 people)
  │
  ▼ (30% conversion - email-gate for dynamic PDF report)
 12% - Lead Captured / Email Submitted (120 people)
  │
  ▼ (15% of leads have >$500/mo savings and click the CTA)
 1.8% - Consultation Booked / Credex Call Scheduled (18 people)
  │
  ▼ (30% sales close rate - matching discounts to their stack)
 0.54% - Credit Purchase / Paying Customer Closed (5.4 customers)
```

**Funnel Yield:** For every 1,000 organic visitors, we close **5.4 active startups**, generating **$97,200 in LTV** at $0 ad spend!

## 4. The Path to $1,000,000 ARR in 18 Months

To hit $1M ARR ($83,333 net margin revenue per month) within 18 months, we calculate the required pipeline volume:

- **Required Monthly Net Margin Revenue:** $83,333 / month.
- **Net Margin Revenue per Closed Startup:** $750 / month.
- **Required Active Closed Startups:** $83,333 / $750 = **111 active startups** on the Credex platform.
- **Startups Closed per Month (over 18 months):** 111 / 18 = **6.2 new closed startups per month**.
- **Required Consultations per Month:** 6.2 / 0.30 (close rate) = **21 consultations/mo**.
- **Required Captured Leads per Month:** 21 / 0.15 (threshold rate) = **140 captured leads/mo**.
- **Required Audits Completed per Month:** 140 / 0.30 (email-gate rate) = **466 completed audits/mo**.
- **Required Unique Visitors per Month:** 466 / 0.40 (completion rate) = **1,165 unique visitors/month**.

### Summary of Requirements:
To achieve $1,000,000 in ARR, the AI Spend Audit Tool needs to attract just **1,165 unique visitors per month** (approx. **39 visitors per day**). This makes the commercial goal highly achievable and realistic through pure organic execution.
