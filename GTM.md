# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy — AI Spend Audit Tool

This document outlines the zero-budget distribution roadmap to acquire **100 high-intent startup leads in 30 days** and funnel them directly into **Credex's** institutional credit pipeline.

---

## 1. Exact Target Persona Definition
Our absolute ideal user is a **technical decision-maker** inside early-stage, VC-backed, or bootstrapped startups:
- **Primary Titles:** Chief Technology Officer (CTO), Founder & CEO, Lead Engineer, VP of Engineering, or Head of Platform.
- **Company Stage:** Pre-seed to Series A startups (team sizes: 5 to 50 developers).
- **Core Problem:** The startup has scaled past the initial prototype phase and is facing mounting billing alerts. They are spending between **$1,500 and $10,000/mo** on AI subscriptions and APIs, but have no centralized oversight. They are beginning to worry about cash runway.

## 3. The 30-Day Zero-Budget Playbook
To get **100 validated leads** without spending a single dollar, we execute a highly tactical content-led acquisition flow:

### Phase A: Value-first Native Posting (Days 1–10)
- **The Strategy:** Do not spam links. Instead, write rich, long-form text posts sharing our **verified pricing dataset** and cost-reduction logic natively.
- **Sample Hook:** *"We analyzed the pricing models of the top 8 AI developer tools (Cursor, Claude, Copilot, ChatGPT, Gemini, v0) as of May 2026. Here are the 4 most common areas where startups are overpaying..."*
- **Action:** Share these analyses on Reddit (`r/startups`, `r/saas`) and Hacker News. At the very end of the educational post, drop a casual link: *"We built a free, no-login audit calculator to inspect your stack instantly: [Link]"*.

### Phase B: Viral Twitter Thread "Stack Roast" (Days 11–20)
- **The Strategy:** Launch a Twitter thread using highly visual comparison charts (e.g. Cursor Pro + GitHub Copilot overlap).
- **Interactive Mechanic:** *"Startups: drop your monthly AI tool bill in the comments, and I will run it through our audit engine to tell you exactly how to save $200-$1,000/mo within 5 minutes. Or run it yourself for free: [Link]"*.
- **Impact:** Each audit results page is highly shareable, featuring unique public URLs with pre-formatted Open Graph tags that show dynamic saving numbers (e.g. *"I just saved $450/mo on my AI stack!"*).

### Phase C: Cold YC & LinkedIn Outreach (Days 21–30)
- **The Strategy:** Target CTOs at startups that recently raised seed funding. 
- **Script:** *"Hey [Name], congratulations on the round! I saw you are building in the [use case] space. We built a free, no-login audit tool that checks if your engineering team is double-subscribed to Cursor/Copilot or overspending on API limits. Takes 30 seconds to run. If you save >$500/mo, Credex can route you discounted institutional credits. Check it out: [Link]"*.
